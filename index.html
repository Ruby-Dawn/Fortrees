George Forte - Local Arborist

Hi, I’m George Forte, your local, fully qualified arborist based in Tamahere, Hamilton. After graduating from Wintec, I launched this business to offer professional, reliable tree maintenance across the Waikato.


No job is too big, too small, or too complex. I am fully equipped with my own climbing gear and chainsaws, allowing me to safely handle everything from high-climb prunes to ground-level clearing. Because I operate independently and source heavy machinery like woodchippers only when needed, I keep overheads low and pass those savings directly on to you.
