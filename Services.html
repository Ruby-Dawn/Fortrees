Services

Tree Pruning & Maintenance

Keep your trees healthy, safe, and looking great year-round. George handles all aspects of pruning, from precision ground-level shaping and hedge trimming to high-altitude canopy maintenance. Proper pruning improves sunlight, clears property lines, and removes dangerous deadwood before it causes damage.


Advanced Tree Work

Managing large trees in tight spaces requires specialised skills. Using professional climbing gear and advanced rigging techniques, George carefully dismantles large or hazardous trees section by section. This controlled method protects your surrounding garden and fences.


Tree Felling

When a tree becomes a safety hazard or needs to clear space for a new project, we provide straight-forward, efficient felling. George utilizes precise directional felling techniques to bring trees down safely and efficiently, ensuring complete control over the drop zone from start to finish.
